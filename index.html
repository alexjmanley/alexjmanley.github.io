Elevator Speech

Hi, my name is Alex Manley and I help individuals and businesses save money by repairing their technology. Before I started working at where I work now, my boss was sending his Ipad and macbook repairs to a repair shop an hour away from where we work. The average repair for an m2 2022 macbook is anywhere from $300-800, and the average repair for a 10th gen Ipad is anywhere from $100-300. Plus the drive to drop off and pick up any devices that were at the shop to be repaired. After I started working for a while I realized this pain point for my boss and I told him that I can do these repairs in house. By sourcing parts for $200 or less and completing repairs in under an hour I am able to significantly reduce both the cost and time it takes to do these repairs. Now instead of overpaying for repairs I can save you money.
